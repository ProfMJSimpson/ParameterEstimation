Morphogen gradient model¶

Long-time BVP $t \to \infty$¶

\begin{equation*} \large{D\dfrac{\textrm{d}^2 U}{\textrm{d}x^2}-kU=0}, \quad \textrm{on} \quad \large{0 < x < \infty}. \end{equation*}

Boundary conditions¶

\begin{align*} \large{J} &\large{=-D\dfrac{\textrm{d}^2 U}{\textrm{d}x^2}} \quad \textrm{at} \quad \large{x=0}, \notag \\ \large{U} &\large{= 0,} \quad \textrm{as} \quad \large{x \to \infty.} \notag \\ \end{align*}

Exact solution¶

\begin{equation*} \large{U = \alpha \textrm{exp}\left(-\beta x \right)}. \end{equation*}

Parameters¶

\begin{equation*} \large{\theta = \left(\alpha = \dfrac{J}{\sqrt{kD}}, \beta= \sqrt{\dfrac{k}{D}}\right)^\top}. \end{equation*}
