Modelling with PDEs

Advection-diffusion equation for density $u(x,t) \ge 0$

\begin{equation*} \large{\dfrac{\partial u}{\partial t} = D \dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} - v \dfrac{\partial u}{\partial x},} \quad \textrm{on} \quad \large{ -\infty < x < \infty.} \label{eq:ADEPDE1} \end{equation*}

Initial condition

\begin{align*} \large{u(x,0)} &\large{= u_b,} \quad \large{x > |h|}, \notag \\ \large{u(x,0)} &\large{= u_b+u_0,} \quad \large{x < |h|.} \notag \\ \end{align*}

Exact solution

\begin{equation*} \large{u(x,t) = u_b + \dfrac{u_0}{2}\left[\textrm{erf}\left(\dfrac{h-(x-vt)}{2\sqrt{Dt}}\right)+\textrm{erf}\left(\dfrac{h+(x-vt)}{2\sqrt{Dt}}\right) \right]}, \end{equation*}

Parameters

\begin{equation*} \large{\theta = (u_0,h,D,v)^\top}. \end{equation*}

Noise model: Log-Normal

\begin{equation*} \large{u^{\textrm{o}}(x_i,t) \mid \theta = \left[u(x_i,t) \mid \theta\right]\eta_i,} \quad \large{\eta_i \sim \textrm{log-normal} (0,\sigma^2)} \end{equation*}

Equivalently

\begin{equation*} \large{u^{\textrm{o}}(x_i,t) \mid \theta = \left[u(x_i,t) \mid \theta)\right]\textrm{exp}(\varepsilon),} \quad \large{\varepsilon \sim \mathcal{N}(0,\sigma^2)} \end{equation*}

Log-likelihood function

\begin{equation*} \large{\ell(\theta \mid u^\textrm{o}(x_i,t)) = \sum_{i=1}^{I} \log \left[\phi\left(u^\textrm{o}(x_i,t); \log(u(x_i,t)), \sigma^2 \right)\right]}. \end{equation*}
